My Background

Printed Circuit Board Design

Began a career in electronics manufacturing in 1991 as a Computer Aided Manufacturing Engineer. This involved working on Unix systems, such as HPUX and Solaris.

Account Manager

In 1998 I was offered a position in outside sales. At the time I was based in San Jose. My position required cold calling and acquiring customers ranging from start-ups to large corporations.

PCB Artist Project Manager

In 2007 I was hired by a manufacturing company to create a new project called PCB Artist. The company had acquired a design software with a captive output only they could manufacture. My responsibilies in this project were many:

  • Learn the software
  • Work with our own developers to create automated ordering of the designs
  • Work with the original developers in the UK
  • Help Vice President of sales in marketing with marketing campaigns
  • Assist Engineering department with the ordered designs
  • Create help content and publish it to the web
  • Assist Production Department with designs in progress
  • Provide tech support to all new customers until new employees were hired to fill that role
  • Train the new staff added to the division
  • Ultimately responsible for the entire new department

Epicodus

Currently attending Epicodus to re-enter the world of tech. This time on the software side. I believe my experience in sales, the business world of the tech and people skills will be valuable in this endeavour as well.
